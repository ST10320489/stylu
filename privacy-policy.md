# Privacy Policy for Stylu

**Effective Date:** November 17, 2025  
**Last Updated:** November 17, 2025

## Introduction

Stylu ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Stylu (the "App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

---

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

We collect the following personal information that you voluntarily provide when using the App:

**Account Information:**
- Email address (required for authentication)
- Name (optional)
- Phone number (optional)
- Profile photo (optional)

**User-Generated Content:**
- Clothing item photos you upload
- Outfit combinations you create
- Calendar events and outfit schedules
- Notes and descriptions you add

### Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we automatically collect:

**Device Information:**
- Device model and manufacturer
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- Mobile network information

**Location Information:**
- Approximate location (city/region level only)
- Used exclusively for weather forecasting
- Not tracked continuously
- Can be disabled in device settings

**Usage Information:**
- App features you use
- Time spent in the App
- Error logs and crash reports
- Performance metrics (anonymized)

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

### Core App Functionality
- **Authentication:** Create and manage your account
- **Wardrobe Management:** Store and organize your clothing items
- **Outfit Creation:** Save and retrieve your outfit combinations
- **Calendar Planning:** Schedule outfits for specific dates
- **Weather Integration:** Provide location-based weather forecasts
- **Notifications:** Send weather alerts and outfit reminders

### App Improvement
- **Analytics:** Understand how users interact with the App (anonymized data)
- **Bug Fixes:** Identify and resolve technical issues
- **Performance:** Optimize app speed and reliability
- **Feature Development:** Develop new features based on usage patterns

### Communication
- **Service Updates:** Notify you of important changes or updates
- **Support:** Respond to your inquiries and support requests

---

## How We Share Your Information

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We share your information only with the following service providers necessary to operate the App:

### Third-Party Services

**Supabase (Database & Authentication)**
- **Purpose:** Cloud database hosting and user authentication
- **Data Shared:** Email, name, phone, wardrobe data, outfits, calendar events
- **Security:** All data encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Privacy Policy:** [https://supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy)

**Firebase (Google LLC) - Push Notifications**
- **Purpose:** Deliver weather alerts and outfit reminders
- **Data Shared:** Device ID, notification preferences
- **Privacy Policy:** [https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

**Remove.bg (Kaleido AI GmbH) - Background Removal**
- **Purpose:** Remove backgrounds from clothing photos
- **Data Shared:** Clothing item images only (no personal information)
- **Privacy Policy:** [https://www.remove.bg/privacy](https://www.remove.bg/privacy)

**OpenMeteo - Weather Data**
- **Purpose:** Provide weather forecasts
- **Data Shared:** Approximate location coordinates only
- **Privacy Policy:** [https://open-meteo.com/en/terms](https://open-meteo.com/en/terms)

**Render (Deployment Platform)**
- **Purpose:** Host our backend API
- **Data Shared:** All data transmitted through our API
- **Privacy Policy:** [https://render.com/privacy](https://render.com/privacy)

### Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., court orders, government agencies).

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

**Technical Safeguards:**
- **HTTPS Encryption:** All data transmitted over secure connections
- **Password Security:** Passwords hashed using bcrypt (never stored in plain text)
- **Database Security:** Access restricted with authentication
- **Biometric Data:** Stored locally on your device only, never transmitted

**Organizational Safeguards:**
- Regular security audits
- Access controls limiting who can view data
- Secure development practices

**Important Note:** No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services.

**Account Deletion:**
- You can delete your account at any time through the App settings
- Upon deletion, all your data will be permanently removed within 30 days
- Backups may be retained for up to 90 days for disaster recovery purposes
